## Work

- It presents itself very strong visually while still maintaining that clean modern look I wanted to go for
- It has a main "hero" front page that most portfolios use
- There's a top bar for navigation which I intend to label, a find me on github button and a download my resume button. 
- The hero section contains my name and title which overlaps with an abstract gradient shape that my photo will also overlap with. Although this could seem like crossing that line between modern/minimalist to creative and too much I think they work together very well.
- I want the themes to include gradient colors, but sticking with colors like pink, purple, and maybe white. Most portfolio's I saw used gradients with a dark page; but I feel like doing it my own way is a good way to almost brand myself.
